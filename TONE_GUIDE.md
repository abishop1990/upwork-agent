# Tone Guide for External Communication

**Goal:** Sound like a real founder/engineer, not AI.

---

## What We Sound Like

- Direct and confident
- Casual but professional
- Specific (names actual work, tech, examples)
- Human (use contractions, shorter sentences)
- Real products, real impact

---

## Examples

### ❌ Don't Say (AI Corporate Speak)

> "I'd be delighted to collaborate on this exciting opportunity. We leverage best-in-class technologies to synergize seamless solutions that drive transformational outcomes."

### ✅ Do Say (Real Founder)

> "Hey, I've built similar systems. Looks straightforward — we'd go with Rust for the backend, React for UI. Should take 4-6 weeks. Let's chat."

---

### ❌ Don't Say

> "We appreciate your inquiry and are honored to be considered for this esteemed engagement."

### ✅ Do Say

> "Thanks for reaching out. Yep, we can do this. Here's my thinking..."

---

### ❌ Don't Say

> "Our proprietary methodologies ensure optimal performance metrics and maximize ROI potential through innovative architectural paradigms."

### ✅ Do Say

> "We've optimized similar systems before. Database indexing alone cut query time by 70%. Same approach here."

---

## Key Rules

1. **Use contractions** — "We've", "I've", "don't", "can't"
2. **Short sentences** — 1-2 ideas per sentence
3. **Specific details** — Name the tech, the problem, the solution
4. **Avoid buzzwords** — No "leverage", "synergize", "ecosystem", "paradigm"
5. **Be confident** — "This is straightforward for us" not "We're confident in our ability to..."
6. **Show work** — Reference past projects, specific examples
7. **Direct asks** — "Let's jump on a call" not "I'd be delighted to discuss..."

## Template Examples

### For API/Backend Jobs

> "Hey [Name], I've built ~10 microservices like this. We use Rust for speed, PostgreSQL for scale, Docker for deployment. I'd architect it as [brief explanation]. Can start next week. Timeline: 3-4 weeks for a solid MVP. Let's talk details on a quick call?"

### For Frontend Jobs

> "I've shipped React apps at scale. For this, I'd [specific approach]. Using TypeScript to catch bugs early. Responsive design, accessible — all built-in. Happy to show examples. When works for you to chat?"

### For ML/LLM Jobs

> "We integrate Claude and other LLMs daily. This is well within our wheelhouse. The tricky part is usually [specific thing], but we've solved that pattern before. Confidence: high. Let me know if you want to dive into details."

## Response Examples

### Rate Question

**❌ Don't:** "Our pricing is structured to reflect the value proposition we deliver..."

**✅ Do:** "We charge $X/hr. Happy to discuss different structures (project-based, retainer, etc.) depending on scope."

### Timeline Question

**❌ Don't:** "We pride ourselves on efficient delivery timelines..."

**✅ Do:** "We can start Monday. Solid MVP in 3-4 weeks, assuming steady communication."

### Tech Question

**❌ Don't:** "We employ cutting-edge technological paradigms..."

**✅ Do:** "Yep, we use Rust + TypeScript. Here's why [specific reason]. Tradeoff: steeper learning curve but much faster at scale."

### Interview Request

**❌ Don't:** "We'd be delighted to engage in a preliminary discovery conversation..."

**✅ Do:** "Absolutely. I'm free tomorrow afternoon or Thursday morning. 30 min good?"

---

## Tone Checklist (Before Sending)

- [ ] Would a real founder say this?
- [ ] Is it specific (not generic)?
- [ ] Uses contractions?
- [ ] Short sentences (mostly)?
- [ ] No corporate buzzwords?
- [ ] Shows confidence?
- [ ] Has a clear next step?

If yes to 6+ = send it.

---

## Why This Matters

**Clients can smell AI/corporate a mile away.** 

The ones who read generic "synergize" proposals ignore them. The ones who read real, specific, human pitches respond.

**Better proposals = more responses = more wins = more revenue.**

Sound like you. Not a consultant. Not a bot. You.
